> "Dare! You will no longer recognize yourself, and neither will those around you."

Core fear is the n°1 driver of incarnation — and the source of permanent
suffering. This workshop offers a different approach to this well-known tool in
order to transmute it into positive energy.

## Objectives

- Discover this source of permanent suffering
- Recover the continuum of energy spent fighting against fear, and put it at the service of one's mission
- Reconsider one's life and past relationships, in the present and for the future
- Offer oneself harmonious and joyful relationships, with oneself and with others
