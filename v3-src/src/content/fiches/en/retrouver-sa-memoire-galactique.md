## End of a cycle

We are living neither a crisis nor a mere societal revolution: we are emerging
from a matrix put in place over millennia. The end of this first timeline opens
the creation of a second one — the "return from the future" to take part in the
new Earth, supported by the intervention of extra- and inner-Earth
consciousnesses.

## Twenty years of experiences

Since 2001, numerous contacts and astral journeys: Greys, plasmic Beings,
Draco reptilian, Pleiadians. Decisive experiences — the redemption of a Draco
in 2018 that put an end to a phobia, the intergalactic contact of 2025
revealing her origins in this galaxy.

## A life testimony

Revelation of her multidimensionality at Mount Shasta, between 2017 and 2019,
with the help of the Inner-Earth beings of TELOS — the return of a version of
herself to collaborate on the new space-time line.
