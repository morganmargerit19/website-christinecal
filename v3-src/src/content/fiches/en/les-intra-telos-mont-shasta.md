Mount Shasta, a 4,700-meter mountain in California, is one of the most
powerful vortexes on Earth. Beneath the mountain, in 5D, in the city of TELOS
("Communication with the Spirit"), live the survivors of Lemuria.

## A connection since 2003

The trigger: reading, in a single night in February 2003, the *Revelations of
the New Lemuria*. It connects Christine to a Telosian who initiates her into
long-distance astral travel and into the construction of her Soul's vehicle.
Twelve journeys to Mount Shasta will follow, along with the organization of
initiatory seminars between 2014 and 2018, in channeling with Master Adama.

## Experiencing multidimensionality

Over the course of the journeys, in a specific place: believing she is
accessing her past in Lemuria, the interaction with the Telosians propels her
"into her future, facing a version of herself who lives with them."

> "Stop dreaming of 5D and decide to live it, for it is your true nature." —
> *a Sage of Telos*

## The new Telos

In 2021, during the astral journeys, the city is found empty and partly
destroyed. Contacts resume in 2022 with the discovery of the new city,
verified on site during the journey of 2026. A book is currently being
written: *Mount Shasta and the New Telos*.
