Mount Shasta, a 4,700-meter mountain in California and considered sacred by
Native Americans, carries the energy of "The One who lives by Divine Law." One
of the most powerful vortexes on Earth, it allows the survivors of Lemuria to
ascend: they live beneath the mountain, in 5D, in the city of TELOS
("Communication with the Spirit"), capital of the inner-Earth cities of Agartha.

## In connection with Telos since 2003

**The trigger** — reading, in a single night in February 2003, the *Revelations
of the New Lemuria (Vol. 1)* by Aurelia Louise Jones and Dianne Robbins
(Éditions Ariane). Deeply moved, Christine is connected to a Telosian who takes
her on a visit of the city in astral travel. These visits and contacts will last
for years; this Telosian initiates her into long-distance astral travel and into
the construction of her Soul's vehicle.

**Successive encounters**

- March 2003 — meeting with Jean-Claude Genel.
- June to October 2003 — meeting with Aurelia Louise Jones in Geneva, Paris and Toulouse.
- Summer 2004 — first journey to Mount Shasta with J.-C. Genel.
- August 2017 — meeting with Dianne Robbins.
- Then 12 journeys to Mount Shasta.

## Organizer of initiatory seminars (2014-2018)

Channeling with the Telosians — including a Great Sage of Telos, Master Adama —
and Ascended Masters. Between 2015 and 2018, organization of International
Encounters with Telos Canada, Telos Israel, Telos Australia and the members of
the World Telos Foundation (entirely independent of the Telos France
association).

## Experiencing multidimensionality

Over the course of the journeys, in a specific place: believing she is accessing
her past in Lemuria, the interaction with the Telosians and the energetic charge
propel her "into her future, facing a version of herself who lives with them."

> "There is a part of us within you." "We are your future." — *the Telosians*

> "Stop dreaming of 5D and decide to live it, for it is your true nature." —
> *a Sage of Telos*

Science and mysticism converge: in 2017, Swiss and Belgian neuroscientists
discovered multidimensional structures in the human brain, billions of neurons
organized across 7 or even 11 dimensions.

## ✦ The new Telos

**Invasion of Telos in 2021 and the end of the galactic war.**

- **2021** — during the astral journeys, the city is found empty and partly destroyed. A video by Elena Danaan (archaeologist, emissary of the Galactic Federation of Worlds) corroborates the observations; encounters confirming the exchanges in July 2024 and August 2025.
- **2022** — contacts resume and the new city of Telos is discovered.
- **2026** — journey to Mount Shasta and more precise connections for on-site verification; meeting with the Telos International group.

Book currently being written: *Mount Shasta and the New Telos*.
