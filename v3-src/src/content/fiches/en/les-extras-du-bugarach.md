The Bugarach Peak, in the south of France near Rennes-le-Château, has
always fueled legends and questions: treasures, UFOs, the "frenzy" of
December 21, 2012. An "upside-down" mountain — in geology as in energy.

> Yesterday's fiction corresponds to today's reality.

## A journey of experiences

- **2003** — A disturbing encounter with an American couple at the foot of the mountain, around a message concerning TELOS.
- **2010** — A visit during astral travel: a place inhabited by non-humanoid beings, a very metallic energy.
- **Late 2025** — "You will go and see the mountain." Summoned to the site by a part of herself.
- **March 20, 2026** — Contact with Guardians: "We were waiting for you…"

## Connection for the New Earth

On a mission for the moment — update in autumn 2026. A workshop "The New
Bugarach" is planned for 2027, and a book of the same title is currently
being written.

> An invitation to questioning: inform yourself before judging, and uphold
> respect for everyone's freedom of thought.
