The Extras of Bugarach and its new energies

An atypical and legendary place, where yesterday's fiction meets today's reality.

An atypical and legendary place

This 1,230-metre mountain, located in the south of France near the mythical site of Rennes-le-Château, in Cathar country, has always fuelled literature and questioning: treasures, the SS, UFOs, or the “frenzy” of December 21, 2012. It even became the subject of a fantasy fiction TV series in 2024, backed by the Occitanie region, in which the entanglement of worlds is clearly revealed — a portrayal that resonates in the current context of official disclosures about UFOs and E.T.s.

Yesterday’s fiction matches today’s reality! Everything has been shown in the films; they often show reality, yet we go on living in illusion.

An “upside-down” mountain — in geology (upper layers older than the inner ones), but also in energy, according to my own experiences.

2003 — A disturbing encounter

At the foot of Bugarach, an American couple. “I was waiting for you!” she tells me. The woman, dressed as a Native American, hands me something concerning TELOS (the book had only just come out), whose trace I would find again on the physical plane at Shasta in 2017. “Where did you put it?” he says — the man unknowingly refers to one of my dreams from 2001 about my Cathar life; a dream that would lead me on the journey of the castles in 2003.

2010 — A visit during astral travel

From a distance, I project myself in astral travel for a visit I hardly appreciated: a place inhabited by various non-humanoid beings, experiments conducted in laboratories, a very metallic energy. An inner duality that reflects an outer reality of humanity. A piece of information is given to me: the need to wait for a change orchestrated by other intelligences.

Some liken Mount Bugarach to Mount Shasta. After more than a dozen journeys to Mount Shasta, I can say that these two mountains are completely different.

2022 — DVDs filmed at Bugarach

A couple of friends dear to my heart take me there. I notice that the energy is different. I come closer, but I remain doubtful.

Late 2025 — “You will go and see the mountain”

To my great surprise, I am “summoned” to this place by a part of myself.

2026 — Contact with Guardians

Experience of March 20, 2026: “We were waiting for you…” On a mission for the moment — update at the end of the summer.
